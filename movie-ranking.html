My top three movies of all time

Red Sparrow

The protagonist of this movie is Jennifer Law. She plays a ballerina that is tricked by her uncle to get involved in drug dealing.

Joker

Joaquin Phoenix is the protagonist in the Joker. He plays a mentally ill young man that has a lot of trouble fitting in society.

Dogman

Dogman is a movie that is similar to the Joker. The story is about a man that was abused as a child and always tried to please his parents.
